The Majestic Hippo

The hippopotamus also called the hippo, common hippopotamus, or river hippopotamus, is a large semiaquatic mammal and ungulate which is mostly herbivorous and native to sub-Saharan Africa. It is of only two extant species in the family Hippopotamidae, the other being the pygmy hippopotamus, (Choeropsis liberiensis or Hexaprotodon liberiensis). Its name comes from the ancient Greek for "river horse".
